Setting up **automated day-trading systems** requires reliable, high-performance platforms offering robust analysis tools. Several platforms are recognized in the industry for their automated trading capabilities. Here are some of the best platforms for automated day trading:

1. **MetaTrader 4 and MetaTrader 5 (MT4/MT5)**: These platforms are widely recognized for Forex trading, but are also used for other instruments. They offer an integrated environment for developing, testing, and implementing automated strategies, thanks to their MQL scripting language.
2. **NinjaTrader**: NinjaTrader is highly appreciated by traders for its flexibility. It enables the creation of automated strategies using C# and offers solid backtesting and analysis tools.
3. **TradeStation**: This platform is renowned for its robustness and analysis tools. **TradeStation** uses its own scripting language, called EasyLanguage, which is accessible enough for beginners.
4. **Interactive Brokers (TWS with API)**: Interactive Brokers is one of the most reputable brokers in the world, and they offer a solid API for those wishing to automate their trading strategies.
5. **QuantConnect**: Cloud-based, this platform allows users to design, backtest, and deploy algorithmic trading strategies. It is integrated with multiple brokers and supports multiple programming languages.
6. **cAlgo**: This is cTrader's algorithmic trading platform. It allows traders to build customized trading robots and indicators using C#.
7. **ThinkOrSwim**: Offered by TD Ameritrade, **ThinkOrSwim** has a scripting environment called thinkScript that allows custom strategies to be developed.
8. **Quantopian**: Although **Quantopian** has closed its service for external investors, its platform is still a valuable tool for developing, testing, and refining algorithmic trading strategies using Python.
9. **Backtrader**: This is a Python platform for researching and backtesting strategies. **Backtrader** is particularly appreciated for its flexibility and ease of use.
10. **AlgoTrader**: A professional solution for institutional and quantitative traders, **AlgoTrader** offers a complete suite for the creation, backtesting, and deployment of automated strategies.

In conclusion, choosing the best platform for **automated day-trading systems** will depend on your specific needs, your programming skills, and the assets you plan to trade. It is always advisable to test the platform with a demo account before deploying a live strategy.
