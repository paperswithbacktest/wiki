## What is a cryptocurrency?

A cryptocurrency is a type of digital or virtual money that uses a technology called blockchain to keep track of transactions. Unlike regular money, like dollars or euros, cryptocurrencies are not controlled by banks or governments. Instead, they are managed by a network of computers all over the world. People use cryptocurrencies to buy things online, invest, or send money to others.

The most famous cryptocurrency is Bitcoin, which was created in 2009. Since then, many other cryptocurrencies, like Ethereum and Litecoin, have been created. You can buy cryptocurrencies on special websites called exchanges, using regular money. Once you have them, you store them in a digital wallet, which can be an app on your phone or a program on your computer. Cryptocurrencies can be a bit risky because their value can go up and down a lot, but many people are interested in them because they offer a new way to handle money.

## What are Bitcoin futures?

Bitcoin futures are contracts that let people bet on what they think the price of Bitcoin will be in the future. They are like agreements where one person agrees to buy Bitcoin at a certain price on a specific date, and another person agrees to sell it at that price. These contracts are traded on special places called futures exchanges, where people can buy and sell them just like stocks.

People use Bitcoin futures to try to make money if they think the price of Bitcoin will go up or down. For example, if someone thinks the price will go up, they can buy a futures contract. If the price does go up, they can sell the contract for more money than they paid. But if the price goes down, they might lose money. Bitcoin futures can be risky, but they also give people a way to manage their risk when they are investing in Bitcoin.

## What is meant by the expiration of Bitcoin futures?

The expiration of Bitcoin futures is when the contract that people made about buying or selling Bitcoin at a certain price ends. This happens on a specific date that was set when the contract was made. When the contract expires, the people who made the agreement have to do what they promised. If someone agreed to buy Bitcoin, they have to buy it at the price they agreed on, and if someone agreed to sell it, they have to sell it at that price.

This can be important because the price of Bitcoin can change a lot. If the price of Bitcoin is different from what was agreed on in the contract when it expires, one person might make money and the other might lose money. ## What trading strategies are commonly used around Bitcoin futures expirations?

Around Bitcoin futures expirations, traders often use a strategy called "position squaring." This means they try to balance their investments so they are not too risky. If they have a futures contract to buy Bitcoin, they might sell some Bitcoin they already have to make sure they don't lose too much money if the price goes down. If they have a contract to sell Bitcoin, they might buy some Bitcoin to be ready to sell it when the contract expires. This helps them manage their risk and be ready for whatever happens to the price of Bitcoin.

This sounds complicated, but it's about making small trades to take advantage of the price changes that happen around futures expirations. Traders might buy and sell Bitcoin quickly to make a little bit of money each time the price goes up or down.
