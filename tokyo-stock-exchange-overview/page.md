## What is the Tokyo Stock Exchange (TSE)?

The Tokyo Stock Exchange (TSE) is a big place in Japan where people buy and sell pieces of companies, called stocks. It started a long time ago in 1878 and is one of the biggest stock markets in the world. The TSE is in Tokyo, the capital city of Japan. Many big Japanese companies, like Toyota and Sony, have their stocks traded there.

The TSE helps companies get money they need to grow by selling stocks to people and other companies. When you buy a stock, you own a small part of that company. The price of stocks can go up or down depending on how well the company is doing and what is happening in the world. The TSE has rules to make sure trading is fair and safe for everyone.

## How was the Tokyo Stock Exchange founded and when did it start operating?

The Tokyo Stock Exchange was founded on May 15, 1878. It was started by the Japanese government to help businesses grow and to make Japan's economy stronger. At that time, Japan was trying to become more like Western countries, which had big stock markets. So, they decided to create their own stock exchange in Tokyo.

The TSE began operating right after it was founded in 1878. At first, it was a small place where only a few stocks were traded. Over time, it grew bigger and more important. Now, it's one of the biggest stock markets in the world, where people can buy and sell stocks from many different companies.

## What are the main indices of the Tokyo Stock Exchange?

The Tokyo Stock Exchange has two main indices that people use to see how the market is doing. The first one is the Nikkei 225, which is also just called the Nikkei. It tracks the stock prices of 225 big companies in Japan. People look at the Nikkei to get a quick idea of how the Japanese stock market is doing. It's kind of like a report card for the market.

The second main index is the TOPIX, which stands for Tokyo Stock Price Index. The TOPIX looks at the stock prices of all the companies listed on the TSE's First Section, which is where the biggest and most important companies are. The TOPIX gives a broader view of the market because it includes more companies than the Nikkei. Both the Nikkei and TOPIX are important tools for investors to understand the health of the Japanese economy.

## How does the Tokyo Stock Exchange influence the global economy?

The Tokyo Stock Exchange (TSE) is a big part of the world's money system. It's one of the biggest stock markets, so what happens there can affect other markets around the world. When the TSE goes up or down a lot, it can make people in other countries feel more confident or worried about their own markets. For example, if the TSE is doing well, it might make investors in the United States or Europe want to buy more stocks because they think the world economy is strong.

Also, many big Japanese companies that are listed on the TSE, like Toyota and Sony, do business all over the world. If these companies do well and their stock prices go up, it can help the economies of the countries they work in. On the other hand, if these companies have problems and their stock prices go down, it can make the world economy weaker. So, what happens on the TSE can have a big impact on how people and businesses around the world feel about money and investing.

## What types of securities are traded on the Tokyo Stock Exchange?

The Tokyo Stock Exchange (TSE) is a place where different types of securities are traded. The main type of security is stocks, which are pieces of ownership in companies. When you buy a stock, you own a small part of that company. The TSE has stocks from many big Japanese companies like Toyota and Sony, as well as smaller companies.

Besides stocks, the TSE also trades bonds. Bonds are like loans that you give to a company or the government. They promise to pay you back with interest over time. Another type of security traded on the TSE is exchange-traded funds (ETFs). ETFs are like baskets of different stocks or bonds that you can buy and sell easily. They help people invest in a lot of different things at once without having to buy each one separately.

The TSE also has a market for derivatives, which are more complex financial products. Derivatives can be things like futures and options, which are agreements to buy or sell something at a certain price in the future. These are used by investors to manage risk or to try to make money from changes in prices. So, the TSE offers a wide range of securities to help people and businesses invest and manage their money.

## What are the trading hours of the Tokyo Stock Exchange?

The Tokyo Stock Exchange, or TSE, is open for trading from Monday to Friday. The regular trading hours are from 9:00 AM to 3:00 PM Japan Standard Time (JST). This is when most of the buying and selling of stocks happens.

There is also a pre-market session that starts at 8:00 AM JST and ends at 9:00 AM JST. During this time, you can place orders, but they won't be filled until the regular trading session begins. After the regular session, there's a post-market session from 3:00 PM to 6:00 PM JST where some trading can still happen, but it's not as busy as the regular hours.

## What are the listing requirements for companies on the Tokyo Stock Exchange?

To list on the Tokyo Stock Exchange, a company needs to meet certain rules. For the Prime Market, which is for the biggest and most important companies, a company must have at least 22 billion yen in market value of its shares that the public can buy. It also needs to have at least 300 shareholders and a certain amount of money coming in every year. The company has to follow strict rules about how it shares information with the public and how it is run.

For the Standard Market, which is for smaller but still important companies, the rules are a bit easier. A company needs a market value of at least 1 billion yen in shares that the public can buy, and it should have at least 150 shareholders. It also needs to show that it makes enough money and follows the rules about sharing information and running the company well. Both markets want to make sure that companies are strong and honest so that people feel safe investing in them.

## How does the Tokyo Stock Exchange handle market regulations and compliance?

The Tokyo Stock Exchange, or TSE, has strict rules to make sure that trading is fair and safe for everyone. They have a team that watches the market all the time to check if companies and traders are following the rules. If they see something wrong, they can stop trading or even punish the people who broke the rules. The TSE also works with the Financial Services Agency of Japan, which is like a big boss that makes sure all financial markets in Japan are doing things right.

Companies that want to be on the TSE have to follow a lot of rules about how they share information with the public. They need to tell everyone important things about their business quickly and honestly. This helps investors make good choices about buying and selling stocks. The TSE checks that companies are doing this and can take them off the market if they don't follow the rules. This way, the TSE keeps the market trustworthy and protects investors.

## What technological advancements has the Tokyo Stock Exchange implemented to improve trading?

The Tokyo Stock Exchange has made a lot of changes to use new technology to make trading easier and faster. One big change was moving to an electronic trading system. This means that instead of people shouting and writing things down, computers do most of the work. This makes trading faster and helps stop mistakes that can happen when people do things by hand. The TSE also started using something called the arrowhead system, which makes trading even quicker and can handle a lot more trades at the same time.

Another important change is that the TSE now offers things like high-frequency trading. This is when computers make a lot of trades very quickly, sometimes in less than a second. This can help traders make money from small changes in stock prices. The TSE also uses technology to keep an eye on the market and make sure everything is fair. They have special programs that watch for anything strange or wrong, which helps them catch and stop problems before they get too big.

## How has the Tokyo Stock Exchange evolved in response to economic crises?

The Tokyo Stock Exchange has changed a lot over the years to deal with big money problems like economic crises. One big example was the 1980s bubble economy in Japan, where stock prices went way up and then crashed down hard. After that, the TSE made new rules to make sure this kind of thing wouldn't happen again. They started watching the market more closely and made rules about how much money companies could borrow to buy stocks. This was to stop people from taking too many risks that could hurt the whole market.

Another big change came after the global financial crisis in 2008. The TSE worked hard to make trading safer and more stable. They improved their technology to handle more trades and made sure the market could keep running even during tough times. They also started working more with other big stock markets around the world to share information and help each other out. These changes have helped the TSE stay strong and keep people's trust, even when the world's economy is going through tough times.
