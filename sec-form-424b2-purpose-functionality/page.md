## What is SEC Form 424B2?

SEC Form 424B2 is a document that companies file with the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) when they want to sell certain types of securities, like stocks or bonds. It's used for what's called a prospectus supplement, which gives more details about the securities being offered. This form helps investors understand important information about the investment they are considering.

The form includes details like the price of the securities, how many are being offered, and any risks involved. It's an important tool for transparency, making sure that investors have all the information they need to make smart choices. By filing this form, companies make sure they are following the rules set by the SEC, which helps protect investors and keep the market fair.
