## What is SEC Form 18-12B?

SEC Form 18-12B is a document that companies need to file with the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) in the Philippines. This form is used when a company wants to register its securities, like stocks or bonds, for the first time. By filing this form, the company provides important information about its business, finances, and the securities it plans to offer to the public. This helps potential investors make informed decisions.

The form includes details such as the company's history, its management team, financial statements, and how it plans to use the money raised from selling the securities. It's important because it ensures transparency and helps protect investors by making sure they have all the necessary information before they invest. Once the SEC reviews and approves the form, the company can proceed with offering its securities to the public.
