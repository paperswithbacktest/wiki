## Background of Fidessa Group

Fidessa Group, initially established as Intercom Data Systems in 1981, holds an illustrious history within the financial sector, reflecting its growth and adaptation to the evolving market landscape. In 2007, the company adopted the name Fidessa, which resonated more strongly with its market identity and recognition, marking a strategic move to solidify its brand presence.

In 2018, Fidessa Group was acquired by ION Group, a global provider of automation software for financial institutions, enabling the continued expansion and enhancement of Fidessa's offerings. This acquisition underscored the broader industry trend of consolidation and integration, aimed at optimizing technology infrastructure within the financial services arena.

The headquarters of Fidessa are located in London, a pivotal financial hub, which serves as a critical vantage point for leveraging global financial markets. Furthermore, Fidessa's presence is reinforced by its offices strategically positioned in major cities worldwide, ensuring comprehensive support and connectivity for its diverse clientele.

Fidessa has meticulously established itself as a cornerstone in delivering trading, investment, and information technology solutions, continually adapting to the demands of financial institutions. Its platforms are renowned for reliability and innovation, facilitating a spectrum of operations from order management to compliance solutions, aligning with the rapid advancements in trading technologies and methodologies.
