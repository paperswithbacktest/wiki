## What is SEC Form 18-K?

SEC Form 18-K is a document that companies have to file with the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC). It is used by companies that are in the business of mining or natural resources. This form helps investors understand the risks and rewards of investing in these companies. By reading the Form 18-K, investors can see if the company is doing well or if there are issues that might affect its future. It's an important tool for making smart investment choices in the mining and natural resources industry.
