## What is paper trading and why is it important?

Paper trading is when you pretend to buy and sell stocks or other investments without using real money. It's like playing a game where you can practice trading and see how the markets work without risking any of your own money. You use a special account that tracks how your pretend trades would do in the real world, based on actual market prices.

It's important because it helps people learn how to trade without losing money. If you're new to trading, paper trading lets you make mistakes and learn from them without any real financial loss. It's a safe way to get comfortable with the trading platform and understand how different investments behave. This practice can build your confidence and skills before you start using real money, making you a better trader when you're ready to go live.
