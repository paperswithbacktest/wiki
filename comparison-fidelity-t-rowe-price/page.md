## What are Fidelity and T. Rowe Price?

Fidelity is a big company that helps people with their money. They have many services like saving for retirement, investing in stocks, and managing money. People can open accounts with Fidelity to buy and sell investments easily. They also offer advice and tools to help people make good choices with their money.

T. Rowe Price is another company that helps people manage their money. They are known for their mutual funds, which are a way for people to invest in a group of stocks or bonds. Like Fidelity, T. Rowe Price offers accounts where people can save for retirement and invest in different ways. They focus a lot on research to help their customers make smart investment decisions.

Fidelity's fee structure can be very different depending on what you invest in. For their own mutual funds and ETFs, Fidelity often has low or no fees at all. T. Rowe Price's fees are a bit different. They usually charge a fee for managing their mutual funds, which can be around 0.5% to 1% of your investment each year. For retirement accounts, the fees can be similar, but they also depend on what you choose to invest in. T.
