## Professional Career

### Early Internships

Shuhang Lian's career trajectory took him through various roles that honed his skills in quantitative analysis and algorithmic trading. During this period, Shuhang gained valuable experience by assisting in desk research and analyzing internal and external market factors to guide clients' strategic directions.

Shuhang's expertise became increasingly evident during his internship at Clear Creek Capital Partners Ltd in Boston in mid-2021. His work with moving averages and [momentum](/wiki/momentum) factors led to back-tested excess returns, underscoring the viability of his strategies.

In October 2021, Shuhang's journey continued at 3i Capital Research in Boston, where he increasingly streamlined his focus on quantitative research. In this capacity, he honed his skills in data analysis and financial modeling, further bolstering his portfolio of [algorithmic trading](/wiki/algorithmic-trading) techniques.

### Akuna Capital

In March 2022, Shuhang joined Akuna Capital as a Junior Quantitative Risk Analyst, a role that showcased his impressive skillset in algorithmic trading and risk management. His expertise in programming languages such as Python, C++, and R has been instrumental to his success. During his tenure, Shuhang was recognized for his ability to blend quantitative analysis with strategic risk mitigation, eventually being promoted to Quantitative Risk Analyst by February 2024.
