## What is Merrill Edge and who owns it?

Merrill Edge is a service that helps people manage their money. It offers things like online banking, investment options, and advice from financial experts. Merrill Edge is owned by Bank of America, a big bank in the United States. Bank of America bought Merrill Lynch, a company that used to own Merrill Edge, during the financial crisis in 2008. Since then, Bank of America has been in charge of Merrill Edge, making sure it works well and helps people with their money.
