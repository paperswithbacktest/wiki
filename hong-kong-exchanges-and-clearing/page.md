## What is the Hong Kong Exchanges and Clearing Limited (HKEX)?

The Hong Kong Exchanges and Clearing Limited, or HKEX, is a company that runs the stock market in Hong Kong. It helps companies list their stocks and bonds so people can buy and sell them. HKEX also makes sure that all trading is done fairly and safely. It started in 2000 when two big groups, the Stock Exchange of Hong Kong and the Hong Kong Futures Exchange, joined together.

HKEX is important because it connects Hong Kong to the rest of the world. Many big companies from different countries list their stocks on HKEX. This makes it easier for people from all over the world to invest in these companies. HKEX also works with other stock markets in China, like the Shanghai and Shenzhen stock exchanges, to let investors trade between these markets more easily.

## What are the listing requirements for companies on the HKEX?

To list on the HKEX, companies need to meet certain rules. For the Main Board, which is for bigger and more established companies, they need to have been in business for at least three years. They also need to have enough money and a certain amount of shares available for the public to buy. The company has to show its financial records and prove it is making money. Also, it must follow all the laws and rules set by HKEX.

For the GEM Board, which is for smaller and newer companies, the rules are a bit easier. These companies don't need to have been in business for as long, but they still need to show their financial records and have some shares for the public. The GEM Board helps these smaller companies grow by giving them a place to raise money. Both boards make sure companies are honest and follow the rules to keep the market fair and safe for everyone.

First, you tell your broker what you want to do - like buy or sell a certain stock. Your broker then sends your order to the HKEX trading system. ## What are some major milestones in the history of HKEX?

HKEX started in 2000 when two big groups, the Stock Exchange of Hong Kong and the Hong Kong Futures Exchange, joined together. This made HKEX a big company that could handle both stocks and futures trading. Before this, the Stock Exchange of Hong Kong was already important, having been set up in 1891. The Futures Exchange started in 1988. When they came together, it made HKEX a strong player in the global market.

Over the years, HKEX has done many important things. In 2007, they started the first trading link between Hong Kong and mainland China, called the Stock Connect. This let investors from Hong Kong trade stocks in Shanghai, and investors from Shanghai trade stocks in Hong Kong. In 2014, they added the Shenzhen Stock Exchange to this link. HKEX also started the Volatility Index in 2015, which helps people see how much the market might change. These steps have helped HKEX grow and become a big part of the world's financial markets.
