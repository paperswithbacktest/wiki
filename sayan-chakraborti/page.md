## Sayan Chakraborti

Sayan Chakraborti is an accomplished quant at Renaissance Technologies LLC with a distinguished career that spans astrophysics, management consulting, and quantitative finance. His work bridges the realms of scientific research and financial strategy, highlighting his ability to integrate complex data analysis with strategic decision-making.

### Early Life and Education

Sayan Chakraborti was born and raised in India, where he developed an early interest in science and mathematics. He pursued a Bachelor of Science degree in Physics at St. Xavier's College from 2003 to 2006. Showcasing academic excellence, Chakraborti was awarded the prestigious Jagadis Bose National Science Talent Search (JBNSTS) scholarship, an accolade that recognizes and rewards young scientific talents across India.

Following his undergraduate studies, Chakraborti continued his academic journey at the Tata Institute of Fundamental Research (TIFR) in Mumbai, where he earned a Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) in Astronomy and Astrophysics from 2006 to 2012. His research at TIFR focused on building models of supernova interaction with circumstellar matter, utilizing statistical techniques to test these theoretical models against actual data.

### Academic and Research Career

After completing his PhD, Chakraborti became a Junior Fellow at Harvard University, where he was part of the renowned Society of Fellows and an ITC Fellow within the Department of Astronomy. From 2012 to 2015, he refined quantitative models of supernova interactions using data from NASA's Chandra X-ray Observatory. His work involved advanced MCMC techniques, including the Goodman-Weare algorithm, leading to significant collaborations and publications in journals such as *Nature* and *Astrophysical Journal*.

### Transition to Management Consulting and Finance

In 2016, Chakraborti joined McKinsey & Company as a Management Consulting Associate. During his tenure there, he applied his analytical and problem-solving skills to advise clients in the financial services and banking sectors. His work spanned multiple industries, including technology, oil and gas, and pharmaceuticals. At McKinsey, he focused on strategy, risk management, operations, and digital analytics.

### Renaissance Technologies LLC

Sayan Chakraborti joined Renaissance Technologies, one of the most successful quantitative hedge funds, in March 2018. Initially, as a researcher, he focused on quantitative finance, identifying opportunities for superior risk-adjusted returns. By January 2021, he advanced to the position of Principal, where he now plays a pivotal role in the firm's investment strategies, particularly in global macroeconomics, futures trading, and financial risk management.
