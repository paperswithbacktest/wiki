## What is XR Trading?

XR Trading is a way to trade digital currencies, like Bitcoin and Ethereum, using a special platform. This platform uses something called "cross reality" or XR, which mixes the real world with virtual worlds. This means that when you trade, you can use virtual reality (VR) headsets or augmented reality (AR) glasses to see and interact with the trading environment in a more immersive way.

Using XR Trading can make the experience of buying and selling digital currencies more fun and easier to understand. For example, instead of just looking at numbers on a screen, you might see virtual coins floating in front of you. You can reach out and grab them to make a trade. This can help people who are new to trading feel more comfortable and engaged with what they are doing.
