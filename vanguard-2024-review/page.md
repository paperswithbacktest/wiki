## What is Vanguard and what services do they offer?

Vanguard is a big company that helps people save and invest money. They started in 1975 and are known for being one of the first to offer low-cost index funds. This means they help people invest in a lot of different companies at once, which can be safer than picking just one or two. Vanguard is owned by the people who invest with them, which is different from most other companies. This means they focus on helping their investors save money on fees.

Vanguard offers many services to help people with their money. They have different types of accounts like retirement accounts, college savings plans, and regular investment accounts. They also have financial advisors who can give advice on how to invest. Vanguard's website and app make it easy for people to manage their money and see how their investments are doing. They also have tools and information to help people learn about investing and make smart choices.

## How does Vanguard's fee structure look in 2024, and has it changed?

In 2024, Vanguard's fee structure remains focused on keeping costs low for investors.
