## Data Management and Integrity

Data management is a critical component of Wealth-Lab, ensuring traders have access to accurate and reliable information. Wealth-Lab supports a variety of data sources, which encompass historical, real-time, fundamental, and sentiment data, offering traders a comprehensive view of the markets. This versatility is vital for traders aiming to build robust and adaptable trading strategies.

One of the key advantages of using Wealth-Lab is its encompassing data solution known as Wealth-Data. This resource effectively eliminates survivorship bias and data spikes, providing traders with clean, tradeable datasets. Survivorship bias is a common problem in backtesting historical stock data, as datasets often only include companies that are currently existing and ignore those that have failed. By overcoming this bias, Wealth-Lab enables more accurate backtesting, leading to more reliable strategy assessment and development.

The platform offers both built-in and extendable data providers, making data accessibility and reliability highly adaptable, even in volatile market conditions. Built-in data providers cover a wide range of markets and asset classes, delivering essential data directly within the platform. Wealth-Lab also ensures data quality and integrity through automated data updates and rigorous integrity checks. These features ensure that data is consistently up-to-date and credible, which is paramount for executing effective trading strategies.
