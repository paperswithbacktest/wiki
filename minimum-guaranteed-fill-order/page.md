## What is a Minimum Guaranteed Fill Order?

A Minimum Guaranteed Fill Order is a type of order that ensures a trader gets at least a certain amount of a stock or asset when they place a trade. This is helpful for traders who want to make sure they can buy or sell a specific minimum amount, even if the market is very busy or if there are not many people trading that asset at the time.

For example, if a trader wants to buy 1,000 shares of a company, they can use a Minimum Guaranteed Fill Order to make sure they get at least 1,000 shares. This can be important in fast-moving markets where it might be hard to get the full amount of shares they want all at once. By using this type of order, the trader can have more confidence that their trade will be completed as they planned.
