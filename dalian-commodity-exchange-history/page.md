## What is the Dalian Commodity Exchange (DCE)?

The Dalian Commodity Exchange (DCE) is a futures exchange located in Dalian, China. It was established in 1993 and is one of the major commodity exchanges in China. The DCE allows people and companies to buy and sell futures contracts for different products, like soybeans, corn, and plastics. This helps farmers, businesses, and investors manage the risks of price changes in these products.

The DCE plays an important role in China's economy. It helps set prices for many important goods and provides a way for people to trade these goods safely and efficiently. The exchange also helps connect Chinese markets with international markets, making it easier for global businesses to trade with China. This can lead to more stable prices and better economic growth.
