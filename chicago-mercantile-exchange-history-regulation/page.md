## What is the Chicago Mercantile Exchange (CME)?

The Chicago Mercantile Exchange, or CME, is a big marketplace where people buy and sell things like farm products, meat, and money from different countries. It started a long time ago in 1898 and is now one of the biggest places in the world for trading these kinds of things. People use the CME to make deals and set prices for things they will buy or sell in the future.

The CME is important because it helps farmers, businesses, and investors know what prices will be like in the future. This helps them plan better and not lose money if prices change a lot. The exchange also uses computers and technology to make trading fast and easy. Today, the CME is part of a bigger company called the CME Group, which runs several other trading places too.

## When was the Chicago Mercantile Exchange founded?

The Chicago Mercantile Exchange, often called the CME, was founded in 1898. It started as a place where people could trade farm products like butter and eggs. At that time, it was called the Chicago Butter and Egg Board.

Over the years, the CME grew bigger and started trading more things like meat, money from different countries, and even things that can't be touched, like interest rates. Today, it's a very important place where people from all over the world come to make deals and plan for the future.

## How is trading conducted at the CME?

Trading at the Chicago Mercantile Exchange, or CME, happens in two main ways: on the trading floor and through electronic systems. On the trading floor, people called traders shout and use hand signals to buy and sell things like farm products, meat, and money from different countries. This can be exciting and fast-paced, but it's not as common as it used to be. The other way is through computers, where traders use special software to make deals. This is much faster and can be done from anywhere in the world.

The electronic trading system at the CME is called the CME Globex. It's open almost all the time, which means people can trade 23 hours a day, from Sunday evening to Friday afternoon. This helps people from different time zones around the world to buy and sell things when it's convenient for them. The CME Globex makes trading very fast and efficient, and it's the main way most trading happens now. This electronic system helps keep everything organized and makes sure that prices are fair and clear for everyone.

## What are the major regulatory changes that have affected the CME over the years?

Over the years, the Chicago Mercantile Exchange, or CME, has seen many changes in the rules that affect how it works. One big change happened in 2000 with the Commodity Futures Modernization Act.
