## Biography of Scott Butler

Scott Butler is a prominent risk analyst specializing in algorithmic trading, particularly within the fixed income sector. Currently employed at Akuna Capital in the Greater Chicago Area, Butler has devoted his career to mastering the complexities of financial risk management, utilizing his extensive knowledge of asset management and algorithmic methodologies.

## Early Life and Education

Scott Butler was born and raised in the United States, developing an interest in finance early in life. He pursued higher education at Loyola Marymount University, where he obtained a Bachelor of Science degree in Business Administration. His academic journey continued at DePaul University, where he earned a Master of Science degree in Computational Finance. This educational background laid a strong foundation for his deep understanding of quantitative methods and computational tools that are pivotal in the field of algorithmic trading.

## Early Career

Butler's career in finance began with an internship at Merrill Lynch in Beverly Hills, California, during the summer of 2005. This four-month stint provided him with valuable exposure to the workings of a major financial institution and ignited his passion for financial markets.

Following this, from 2006 to 2009, Butler worked as a Senior Cash Control Specialist at Western Asset Management in Pasadena, California. During his three years in this role, he honed his skills in cash flow management, learning the intricacies of asset management that would be crucial in his future roles.

## Professional Experience

### National Futures Association

In May 2013, Butler joined the National Futures Association in Chicago, IL, as a Risk Management Analyst. Over the [course](/wiki/best-algorithmic-trading-courses) of a year and five months, he played a critical role in assessing and managing risks associated with futures trading. This position allowed him to deepen his knowledge of market dynamics and regulatory frameworks, solidifying his expertise in risk management.

### Optiver

Butler's career progressed significantly with his move to Optiver in September 2014, where he served as a Market Risk Analyst. During his tenure of nearly three years, Butler contributed to the development of advanced risk assessment models, leveraging technology to optimize trading strategies. His work at Optiver positioned him as an expert in understanding and mitigating the risks inherent in market-making and proprietary trading, aligning with the latest methodologies in quantitative finance, as outlined in scientific publications like "Algorithmic and High-Frequency Trading" by Álvaro Cartea, Sebastian Jaimungal, and José Penalva.

### Akuna Capital

In November 2017, Scott Butler joined Akuna Capital, a leading trading firm known for its innovative approach to market-making and [algorithmic trading](/wiki/algorithmic-trading). As a Risk Analyst, Butler has been instrumental in advancing the company's risk management frameworks and integrating cutting-edge technologies like [machine learning](/wiki/machine-learning) and blockchain into the trading algorithms. His contributions have been pivotal in enhancing the firm's competitive edge in high-frequency trading environments, echoing insights from renowned works such as "High-Frequency Trading: A Practical Guide to Algorithmic Strategies and Trading Systems" by Irene Aldridge.
