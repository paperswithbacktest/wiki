## What is the Norwegian Krone?

The Norwegian Krone is the official currency of Norway. It is often written as NOK or just krone. People in Norway use it to buy things like food, clothes, and other stuff they need. The krone is also used in some places like Svalbard and Jan Mayen, which are part of Norway.

The krone started being used in 1875. Before that, Norway used different kinds of money. The krone is divided into 100 øre, but øre coins are not used much anymore. The value of the krone can go up or down compared to other countries' money, like the US dollar or the Euro. This can make things more or less expensive for people traveling to or from Norway.
